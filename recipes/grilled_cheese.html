Grilled Cheese

Description

This basic grilled cheese recipe is for cooks with brain damage. You shouldn't really need a recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • a few slices of cheese
  • butter

Steps

  1. Preheat frying pan
  2. Butter the bread on one side
  3. Put one slice in the frying pan, butter side down
  4. Put the cheese on top of the bread in the pan
  5. Put the other piece of bread on top, butter side up
  6. Cook one side a bit, flip, and cook the other side
  7. Cook, flipping to make sure is even browned/li>
  8. Enjoy!
