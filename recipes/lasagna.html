Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is a delicious, cheesy delight that is sure to impress your guests. But if you don't like your guests, there is an alternative ingredient set.!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook and stir beef until brown. Add garlic and saute until well browned. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. Cook the noodles to soften
  4. Starting with a layer of noodle, starrt building alternating layers of ingredients
  5. Top with final layer of sauce, and a layer of cheese
  6. Cook at 375 for 60 minutes, check after 45 , in case you want to change anything
  7. Enjoy!
